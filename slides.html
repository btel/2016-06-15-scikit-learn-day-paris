Software Carpentry as an open source community

Common Characteristics of an Open Source Community

  • inclusive by default and transparent,
  • distributed contributors,
  • diverse motivations (commercial, academic and "philantropic"),
  • working together on an open source "product",
  • communicatig through internet channels,
  • self-organizing meritocracy,
  • scalable hierarchy.
